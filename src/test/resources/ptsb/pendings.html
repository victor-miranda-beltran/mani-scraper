What are pending transactions?

Imagine you went to the cinema on Saturday and paid by card. The cinema mightn't process payments until Monday. Until then we'll show this as a pending transaction.

What are uncleared funds?

Uncleared funds is the name we give to money that we know is due to be deposited or withdrawn from your account. Let’s say you deposit a cheque today, we'll show the funds here until the money has made its way into your account.

For domestic (euro) cheques, it can take 5 working days for funds to clear. For nondomestic cheques it could take a bit longer.
